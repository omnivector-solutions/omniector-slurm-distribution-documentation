Welcome to the documentation for the Omnivector Slurm Distribution!
===================================================================

This documentation serves as the source of truth for information
pertaining to deploying and/or operating the Omnivector Slurm Distribution.

The `slurm-charms <https://github.com/omnivector-solutions/slurm-charms/>`_
are at the heart of our operations suite, and encapsulate the operational
know-how and automation needed to facilitate the lifecycle of a SLURM cluster.

Follow the documentation below to get up and running!
