This documentation is the source of truth for information pertaining to deploying and operating the Omnivector Slurm Distribution.

OSD consists of a set of codified automations called "charms". Charms are collections of custom curated operations that are used to support the "charmed" application lifecycle.

The slurm-charms are at the heart of our operations suite and encapsulate the operational know-how and automation needed to facilitate the lifecycle of a SLURM cluster.

The slurm-bundles define base configurations for the slurm deployments on different clouds.

Follow the documentation below to better understand how to get up and running and take advantage of the full range of features contained in the Omnivector Slurm Distribution!
