Welcome to the documentation for the Omnivector Slurm Distribution!¶

This documentation is the source of truth for information pertaining to the deployment and operation of the Omnivector Slurm Distribution (OSD). The Omnivector Slurm Distribution stands on a suite of codified operations to asssemble, install, deploy, and operate SLURM.
